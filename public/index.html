What is a vehicle safety recall?

A safety recall is issued when a manufacturer identifies a fault that could put drivers, passengers, or other road users at risk. Recalls are free to fix — the manufacturer must repair your vehicle at no cost. But you only find out if you're told. That's what CarRecall is for.
